Our Services

Three integrated One Health services through a single voice interface.

Service 01

🩺 Human Health

AI-powered symptom assessment and primary care guidance in local languages.

Our health assistant provides 24/7 access to medical guidance, helping rural communities with everything from common ailments to emergency coordination.

Key Features:

  • ✓ Symptom Assessment & Triage — AI analyzes symptoms and provides guidance
  • ✓ Primary Care Guidance — First-response medical advice in Setswana
  • ✓ Emergency Coordination — Connects to nearest facilities when needed
  • ✓ Maternal Health & HIV Awareness — Specialized women's health support
  • ✓ Facility Referrals — Direct connections to healthcare providers
Service 02

🐄 Animal Health

Livestock disease diagnosis and veterinary guidance for farmers.

Protect your livestock and livelihood with AI-powered veterinary assistance. Our system helps detect diseases early and provides guidance on treatment and prevention.

Key Features:

  • ✓ Livestock Disease Diagnosis — Photo-based AI analysis of symptoms
  • ✓ Foot-and-Mouth Monitoring — Critical regional disease tracking
  • ✓ Vaccination Scheduling — Automated reminders and tracking
  • ✓ Veterinary Guidance — Expert advice in local language
  • ✓ Herd Health Tracking — Monitor your entire livestock population
Service 03

🌱 Agriculture

Crop disease diagnosis and climate-smart farming advice.

Maximize your harvest with AI-powered agricultural guidance. From disease detection to market prices, we help farmers make informed decisions.

Key Features:

  • ✓ Crop Disease Diagnosis — Take a photo, get instant analysis
  • ✓ Climate-Smart Planting Advice — Adapted to local conditions
  • ✓ Pest Management Guidance — Effective, sustainable solutions
  • ✓ Market Price Information — Know when and where to sell
  • ✓ Weather Alerts — Plan your farming activities
