About Smart Village

Our mission to bring AI-powered One Health services to rural communities across Africa.

Our Mission

Leaving No Community Behind

Smart Village is the first voice-activated, local-language AI providing integrated One Health services through a single rural access point.

We believe that every person, regardless of where they live, deserves access to quality healthcare, veterinary services, and agricultural guidance. Our solar-powered AI kiosks bridge the gap between rural communities and essential services.

By speaking in local languages like Setswana and Kalanga, our AI assistant breaks down the barriers of literacy and language that have historically excluded rural communities from digital health solutions.

Our Approach

The One Health Framework

We recognize that human health, animal health, and environmental health are interconnected.

🩺

Human Health

Primary care guidance, maternal health, HIV awareness, and emergency coordination for rural communities.

🐄

Animal Health

Livestock disease diagnosis, foot-and-mouth monitoring, and vaccination scheduling for farmers.

🌱

Agriculture

Crop disease diagnosis, climate-smart farming advice, and market information for sustainable livelihoods.
