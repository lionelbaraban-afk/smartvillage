How It Works

Voice-first AI technology designed for rural communities.

User Experience

Simple. Accessible. Effective.

No smartphone needed. No internet required. Just speak.

1
🚶

Walk Up

Visit your local Smart Village kiosk — solar-powered and always available.

2
🗣️

Speak

Talk naturally in Setswana, Kalanga, or English. Describe your problem.

3
🤖

AI Responds

Get instant guidance on health, livestock, or crops from our AI assistant.

4
✅

Take Action

Follow guidance or get connected to clinics, vets, or extension officers.

Technology

The Tech Behind the Magic

Cutting-edge AI designed for low-resource environments.

🎤

Voice Recognition

Whisper ASR fine-tuned for African languages including Setswana, Kalanga, and regional dialects.

OpenAI Whisper
🧠

AI Engine

Built on open-source Voxtral (Mistral, Apache-2.0), adapted to One Health protocols, local diseases, and agricultural best practices.

Open-source Voxtral
🔊

Voice Synthesis

ElevenLabs text-to-speech generating natural, culturally appropriate responses in local languages.

ElevenLabs TTS
☀️

Solar Power

Off-grid solar panels with battery storage ensure 24/7 operation regardless of electricity access.

📡

Edge Computing

Local processing with periodic sync means the system works even with intermittent connectivity.

📱

SMS Integration

Follow-up messages, reminders, and alerts sent via SMS to basic feature phones.

Backend Dashboard

Real-Time Intelligence for Authorities

Every kiosk is a health sentinel, feeding anonymized data to local and national authorities.

🦠

Pandemic Surveillance

Human and animal disease outbreak early warning system with geo-tagged alerts.

📊

Predictive Analytics

AI-powered trend analysis to predict health events before they become crises.

🌾

Agriculture Planning

Crop disease patterns and yield predictions for food security planning.

🗺️

Resource Allocation

Data-driven deployment of healthcare workers, vaccines, and supplies.
